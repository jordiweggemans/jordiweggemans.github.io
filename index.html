Jordi Weggemans

I'm a researcher working on quantum computation and information. I hold a PhD in quantum computation and information from the University of Amsterdam (graduated November 2025), and I did my PhD at the Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI) and QuSoft. My research focuses on when and how quantum resources can offer advantages over classical resources in computation, using tools from computational complexity.

Next to research, I write and produce music — mainly pop, neo-soul and cinematic music, with occasional genre experiments — and I’m available for collaborations and for writing/production work.
